What I Learned About Being a Friend

I learned the most about being a friend when I was left alone.

I stood on The Friend's shoulders and shouted,

“I’m here! I’m here! I’m here!”

The Friend, sweaty and weak, echoed still,

“He’s here! He’s here! He’s here!”
